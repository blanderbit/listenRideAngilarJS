Photo tips for making your listing stand out

The first thing prospective riders look at when considering a bicycle on listnride are the photos in the listing overview. The right photo(s) can make all the difference. Listings with good photos will receive more interest and more bookings. We would like to give you a few tips on how to take the perfect shot of your bicycle.

Lighting and Background

Use natural light, set up your shot outside during daylight or indoors near a window or a white wall. Early morning and late afternoon light will produce best results. Keep your background as simple as possible. Try to create a contrast to your bicycle. For example you can place your bicycle in front of a concrete wall.

How to Shoot

Show some perspective of your bicycle, so rather shoot from an angle than a frontal perspective.
